About

This project is maintained by Katherine Cory with the aim of bringing together local web and creative freelancers in the East Midlands.

I've been a freelancer in Derby for six years and I enjoy attending local meet-ups and socialising with fellow creatives and freelancers. I was inspired by the Oxford Freelancers website and wanted to create a space that helped match the right freelancer with the right project in my area. I believe there's plenty of work to go around and we should be supportive of each other! Hopefully, this list will help, if like me, you're often asked for recommendations of other freelancers or even if you need to hire someone to work with you on one of your own projects.

I'm based in Derby but it has quite a small community, as does places like Loughborough and Northampton so I thought it would be ideal to include the whole of the East Midlands so places like Derby, Northampton, Lincoln and Loughborough could unite with Leicester and Nottingham to form a larger community.
